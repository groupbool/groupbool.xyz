Bollywood Bangers

pb • Jan 16, 2020

Bollywood frequently gets written off as a second rate film industry - and for good reason. Most movies are poorly written and laughably acted cashgrabs. But every few years, a new movie comes out that equals if not surpasses Hollywood quality blockbusters. In my opinion, no director is better at producing these films than Sanjay Leela Banshali. His movies draw from epic periods in Indian history (albeit with a good bit of fictionalization and bias).

These movies boast multi million dollar budgets, ensemble casts, and exquisite visuals. These visuals are perhaps at their best during the scenes when these three songs are performed. They feature the main actress(es) performing a dance routine to some hair raising, heart pumping acoustics. The combination of coordinated routines and the striking production makes these songs certified bangers.

Dola Re
Kavita Krishnamurthy
Ghoomar
Shreya Ghoshal
Pinga
