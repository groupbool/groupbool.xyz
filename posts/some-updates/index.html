some updates

we still here
by sp
jan 26, 2023

Hey folks, it’s been a while since we last posted. We’re still around but we’ve all been caught up in our own adventures these past few years.

pb moved to the other coast to start a new job. xv and oc embraced the digital nomad life and have worked from multiple countries. I quit my job and moved ~7k miles (or ~12k km for my metric homies) across the world.

We might have temporarily stopped writing on groupbool however we definitely did not stop listening to music. It was a constant presence wherever we were. Just in 2022 alone, we collectively listened to a total of 22,364 tracks and 9,423 artists.

I re-discovered old artists (definitely not talking about aubrey here) and found a deep appreciation for new genres.

We will be back soon enough.

Stay gold.
